# Usage Disclaimer

Last reviewed: June 28, 2026

This repository provides a small weekly reporting tool for retrieving and
summarizing publicly available market data from Yahoo Finance for personal
tracking and review.

The tool is intended for low-volume, user-initiated weekly use. It is not
designed for high-frequency trading, automated market scraping, investment
execution, or bulk redistribution of financial data.

This repository does not host or redistribute proprietary market datasets,
publisher data feeds, paid financial content, logos, or third-party trademarked
assets. Generated reports may include ticker symbols, prices, dates, and
computed metrics derived from Yahoo Finance data at the time of retrieval.

Users are responsible for complying with Yahoo Finance terms of use, exchange
data rules, fund issuer terms, and applicable laws before using, publishing, or
redistributing generated reports or derived datasets.

Nothing in this repository grants permission to copy, resell, redistribute, or
otherwise commercially exploit Yahoo Finance data or exchange-provided market

controls, or instructions prohibit the intended use.

## No Investment Advice

This project is for informational and personal record-keeping purposes only.
It does not provide investment, financial, tax, legal, or trading advice.

Metrics such as drawdown (DD), week-over-week change (WoW), and 52-week range
